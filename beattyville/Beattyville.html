Beattyville, Kentucky: Home to the Head of the Kentucky River

Beattyvile Facts

  • The Kentucky river starts in Beattyvile
  • The North Fork, Middle Fork, and the South Fork meet to form the Kentucky River
  • Population: 1,307 - 2010 U.S. Census Bureau
  • Ten Minutes from the Red River Gorge
